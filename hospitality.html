Hospitality

Information on Housing, Dining, and Transportation

Housing

The Hampton Inn Hotel is a 5-minute walk from the Student Center, where all the activities are going to be throughout the conference. Every delegate will be staying in this hotel, and check-in will be located in the hotel lobby so settling into their hotel rooms will be a breeze. The basement parking deck is available for delegations to park their vans in. Saturday breakfast will also be provided in the hotel, with alternative breakfast options for people having special dietary needs available as well. There will be a hospitality room in the hotel Friday night, stocked full with food to encourage delegates to bond with each other.

Dining

Friday dinner and Saturday lunch will be catered by our very own Georgia Tech catering, with options accommodating all sorts of dietary needs. Delegates will be eating at the Student Center Greenhouse, which seats 200 people in a room with floor-to-ceiling glass windows.

Transportation and Security

Delegations will be able to park their vehicles in a parking lot adjacent to the hotel or the Student Center, where all the activities will commence. The conference staff is working with GTPD to patrol the surrounding areas to ensure delegate safety.
