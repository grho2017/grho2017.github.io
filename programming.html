How to submit a program

The apocalypse has laid waste upon our society to the brink of oblivion. Now, it is all up to us. In order to survive and thrive during these times, we are calling out for any skilled and courageous survivors (delegates) to stand up and volunteer to hold special gatherings (programs) of great impact.

Special Gathering Track Descriptions

Knowing your Terrain:
In order to thrive, increase efficiency, and maximize the use of resources available, we must learn to grow by increasing our knowledge, diversifying our world view, and developing your expertise.

Leading Your Survivors:
Within all groups of people, all great civilizations, and all functional communities, leadership is essential. This track will focus on how to become a leader, be a leader, train and encourage others to be leaders, all in order to one day rebuild our great nation, GRHO.

Making Alliances:
Unity is one of the keys to success. Learning to work together increase involvement, share your success, and encourage others to be engaged and active within your community will help build stronger bonds and keep your group of survivors together. Examples here would include: Building community, community engagement, diversity within a community, open and safe environments for people to be themselves or be free to express their opinions, and making halls into homes.

Advisor Resource Training:
This will count toward ART certification.

Spirit Points

  • 1 point for submission
  • 2 points if the program is selected
