Programming

Program submission are due January 15th!

During the conference, delegates will be able to attend various programs to equip them to become better leaders on their campuses. There are three programming tracks that represent our vision of building community on our campuses, developing leadership in our constituents, and encouraging the personal growth of our leaders. Program submissions are due on January 15th.

Leadership Development

This track will focus on being a leader and molding others into leaders in your community

Community Building

This track will focus on engaging your immediate team and the wider community

Personal Growth

This track will focus on expanding your horizons on topics like inclusivity and social issues, as well as how to educate your community on these topics
