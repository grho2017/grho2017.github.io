Keynote Speaker

Our keynote speaker will be Dean Stephanie Ray. Dean Ray is honored to have created two Diversity Offices at two Georgia institutions of higher learning. First, Dean Ray is currently the Associate Dean of Students and the Director of the Office of Student Diversity Programs at the Georgia Institute of Technology. Secondly, Dean Ray was the first Director for the Office of Multicultural Programs at Georgia Southern University. Dean Ray is a keynote speaker and workshop facilitator who speaks on a number of areas,including but not limited to diversity and gender issues, leadership development and communication skills. Dean Ray is also a member of Toastmasters International, and she is a certified diversity trainer for the National Coalition Building Institute and recently completed the Diversity Training Institute of the National Conference on Race and Ethnicity in American Higher Education. In addition, she works with a number of corporations to increase their diversity pipeline and has a vast array of clients in Higher Education and corporate America.
